How to use

Twitter:

STEP 1: Enter any username into the searchbar then continue .

STEP 2: View all recent tweets from that username.(Tweets will then be flagged as inappropriate or not)

Reddit:

STEP 1: Enter any subreddit name into the searchbar.

STEP 2: See the top subreddits matching the name.(subreddits will then be flagged as inappropriate or not)

YouTube:

STEP 1: Search for a YouTube vidYouTube and then copy the URL.

STEP 2: Once you got thr URL, paste it into searchbar and then submit.

STEP 3: View all comments from that video.(Comments will then be flagged as inappropriate or not)
